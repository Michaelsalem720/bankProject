Future First Bank Privacy Policy:

Future First Bank values the privacy of its customers and is committed to protecting their personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

Information Collection: Future First Bank collects information from customers in the course of providing banking services. This information may include name, address, phone number, email address, Social Security number, account information, and transaction history.

Use of Information: The information collected by Future First Bank is used to provide banking services, manage customer accounts, comply with legal and regulatory requirements, and for other legitimate business purposes.

Sharing of Information: Future First Bank does not sell or share customer information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers to assist in providing banking services, with law enforcement or other government agencies as required by law, and in other circumstances where necessary to protect the security of customer information.

Data Security: Future First Bank uses various security measures, including encryption and firewalls, to protect customer information. Despite these measures, no system is completely secure and customers are advised to take steps to protect their personal and financial information.

Customer Access: Customers may request access to their information and request corrections if necessary.

Changes to Privacy Policy: Future First Bank may change this Privacy Policy at any time and will provide notice of any changes.
